# Decision Framework

## Decision Categories

### Enterprise Standard Decision
A company-wide choice that many teams should inherit.

### Reference Architecture Decision
A reusable target design that solves a recurring problem.

### Application Decision
A local application choice made within enterprise guardrails.

## Evaluation Criteria

All material decisions should consider:
- business fit
- security and compliance
- operational burden
- cost
- team skills
- scalability
- portability
- integration fit
- community or vendor support
- migration and exit strategy

## Decision Outcomes

- Approved Standard
- Approved With Conditions
- Trial / Limited Adoption
- Not Approved
- Sunset / Migrate Away
