# Architecture Principles

## 1. Business Value First
Technology choices should improve delivery speed, reliability, security, maintainability, or cost effectiveness.

## 2. Open Standards Preferred
Prefer protocols, formats, and interfaces with broad industry support.

## 3. Open Source Preferred When Practical
Use open source products and tooling when they meet business, security, reliability, and support needs.

## 4. Security by Design
Identity, least privilege, secrets handling, logging, and vulnerability management must be designed in from the start.

## 5. Simplicity Over Novelty
Choose the simplest architecture that satisfies the requirement.

## 6. Reuse Before Reinventing
Use approved shared services, reference architectures, and common patterns before creating custom solutions.

## 7. Automate Repeatable Work
Provisioning, testing, deployment, and compliance checks should be automated where feasible.

## 8. Observable Systems
Applications must emit logs, metrics, and health signals that support operations and incident response.

## 9. Document Significant Decisions
Important architecture decisions must be recorded in ADRs.

## 10. Exceptions Are Explicit
Deviations from standards must be documented and approved.
