# User Personas

## Purpose

This document defines the primary user personas for AICRM based on the current application scope and target audience.

## Current State

AICRM is currently designed for a single implicit persona: an individual sales professional or small business owner who manages their own customer relationships locally. The application assumes the user is comfortable with browser-based tools and does not require multi-user collaboration.

### Primary Persona: Individual Sales Professional

- **Role**: Sales representative, account manager, or business owner.
- **Goals**: Track contacts, manage a lead pipeline, log activities, and send templated communications.
- **Tech Comfort**: Moderate to high — comfortable using web applications.
- **Current Pain Points**: Data is trapped in a single browser; no sharing or collaboration; manual backup required.

### Secondary Persona: Small Team Lead

- **Role**: Team lead or manager overseeing a small sales team.
- **Goals**: Visibility into team pipeline, activity tracking, and reporting.
- **Current Pain Points**: Cannot share data with team members; no dashboards or reports beyond the personal view.

## Gaps

- No multi-user persona support exists.
- No admin or IT persona is considered (no user management, no settings beyond personal preferences).
- No persona for compliance or audit roles.

## Target Direction

The target personas will expand to include:

- **Multi-user sales teams** with shared data and role-based views.
- **Administrators** who manage users, roles, and system configuration.
- **Compliance officers** who require audit logs and data export capabilities.
- **IT operations** who deploy, monitor, and maintain the platform.
